The Scots College, Thursday, 19th November, 2015

ScotsFest is a showcase of young filmmaking talent. Now in its eigth year, ScotsFest is open to all school age filmmakers.
Entries close on the 13th of November.

ScotsFest will be held on Thursday the 19th of November 2015, in the Auditorium of The Scots College, Bellevue Hill.
Enter from Victoria Road. Guests are asked to arrive from 5:30pm with program commencing at 6:30pm.

Movie snacks and BBQ food is available.
Entry is free.
For more information, please contact The Scots College (02) 9391 7672.

Le Garcon

The Scots College's Nick Ward and Bailey Thomas contemplate a misfit boy's place in the world, and confronting the things we don't want to confront. In French/Black & White.

Flying in Circles

International Grammar School's Rex Woods creates a world of simpler times, when everything was alright.

Anti Citizen

The Scots College's Mitchell Leslie presents a dystopic future with mind control, oppressive masked police and deserted apartment buildings.

For You

The Scots College's Angus Crichton tells a heartfelt tale of his families heritage and his future.

Entry Rules/Terms and Conditions

The Scotsfest Short Film Festival will be held on Thursday the 19th November 2015,
in the Scots College Main Auditorium, Victoria Road, Bellevue Hill.

Movie Snacks and BBQ food is avaliable.

  • Entries are limited to school students.

  • The use of any copyright music, graphics and video must be acknowledged in credits.

  • Copyright for the submitted films remains the property of the student.

  • Copies of films will be uploaded to the tscdigitalmedia Vimeo page and a link provided to entrants.

  • Film entries may be occasionally used at the discretion of the ScotsFest committee.

  • HD films preferred on USB or DVD. Submissions in 4:3 may be cropped.
